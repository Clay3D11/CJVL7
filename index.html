About Me

I'm a multidisciplinary creator with expertise in frontend development, game design, 3D art, and video editing. My work bridges the gap between interactive technology and visual storytelling — crafting immersive experiences through clean code, dynamic visuals, and cinematic motion.




Limitless Music

At the heart of Universal Limitless lies Limitless Music — a streaming and showcase hub where artists can publish music, music videos, and portfolios in a professional, high-visibility environment. It’s more than a platform; it’s a launchpad for talent.


Oline Store

Our online store connects creators directly with their fans. Artists can sell their official merchandise, while a separate curated shop offers 3D art, prints, and digital visuals for collectors and enthusiasts worldwide.


Limitless Visuals

The brand also features a dedicated visual art store, offering premium 3D artwork, framed prints, and digital visuals for collectors and enthusiasts seeking bold, original designs.
